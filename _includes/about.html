About me

My fullname is NGUYEN Dong Hai Phuong. I have attended iCub Facility, Italian Institution of Technology (IIT), Italy since January, 2016 as PhD Fellow. I also hold Marie Curie Fellowship as an Early Stage Researcher.

My research interest focus on developing the spatial perception and sensorimotor competences of humanoid robotics through self supervised learning, mimicking the baby development at very first stage.

I got Master degree in Advanced Robotics (EMARO) from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, France and University of Genoa, Italy, where I conducted research on Control and Planning for UAV robots.
